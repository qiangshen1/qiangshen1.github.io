Bio
I'm a Ph.D. student at the Institute of Engineering Mechanics, China Earthquake Administration (IEM, CEA). My research focuses on the reproduction/prediction of strong ground motions generated by large earthquakes through numerical simulation techniques.
I'm a Ph.D. student in the Engineering Seismology Research Center (ESRC) at the Institute of Engineering Mechanics, China Earthquake Administration (IEM, CEA). My research focuses on the reproduction/prediction of strong ground motions generated by large earthquakes through numerical simulation techniques, I am also interested in the basin-induced amplification as well as the characteristic effects of near-fault ground motions. Now, I am working in the Wen's Lab .
My mission: Earthquakes don’t kill people—buildings do!
